Google's Women Techmakers program provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology.

This 2023, we are excited to announce the 6th edition of our annual event.

IWD Event will take place on March 25, it will be 100% in-person half-day event in Barcelona and as in previous years we seek to boost and promote the role of women in technologies through a track made up of a series of talks which is designed so that attendees do not miss anything during the event.

¡Nos complace presentar la sexta edición de nuestro evento anual, International Women’s Day.

El IWD Event se realizará el 25 de Marzo y será un evento de media jornada 100% presencial en Barcelona y como en años anteriores buscamos impulsar y promover el rol de la mujer en tecnologías a través un track compuesto de una serie de charlas el cual esta pensado para que los asistentes no se pierdan de nada durante el evento.
