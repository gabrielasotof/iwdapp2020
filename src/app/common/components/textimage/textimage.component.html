Google's Women Techmakers program provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology.

This 2023, we are excited to announce the 6th edition of our annual event.

In our last event we had a full day of speaking breakouts in two tracks (one in Spanish and one in English), with over 150 attendees. This year we will have a lot more surprises, to continue to highlight and expose women talent, and contribute to reducing the industry gap.
